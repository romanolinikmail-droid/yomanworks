Leaperkim Headlight Cutoff

Stop annoying riders and pedestrians with your stock headlight — install my cutoff and people will stop turning away from you. Thanks to its design, the cutoff shroud shapes a clean, controlled beam without using any lenses. For the first time, a tetrahedral inner wall texture is used to diffuse light, eliminating stray internal reflections (s c i e n c e). As a result, there's no parasitic glare, and the shroud won't melt even when placed close to the light source.
